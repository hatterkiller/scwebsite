About Us

The ISB Student Council, is an elected group of students, comprised of four class councils, and a student council cabinet. This years group of participants can be found on the "members" tab.
The Student Council is tasked with both addressing student issues with specific committees, as well as planning and organising events ranging from assemblies to dances. Official minutes recording the committee meetings can be found in the "Meeting Notes" Tab.
If you have any issues to bring forward to the Student Council you may approach any of the members, or simply use the contact page.
